Multi-line math mode should indent but not re-wrap
\(
  1 + 2 + (
    3 + 4
  + 5 + 7 )
  + 8 + 9
\)

\[
  1 + 2 + (
    3 + 4
  + 5 + 7 )
  + 8 + 9
\]

Many environments should also indent but not re-wrap
\begin{equation}
  x + y
  = \sum z_i
\end{equation}

\begin{equation*}
  x + y
  = \sum z_i
\end{equation*}

\begin{align}
  x & y \\
  z & \cdot
\end{align}

\begin{align*}
  x & y \\
  z & \cdot
\end{align*}

\begin{tabular}{cc}
  x & y \\
  z & \cdot
\end{tabular}

\begin{tabular*}{cc}
  x & y \\
  z & \cdot
\end{tabular*}
